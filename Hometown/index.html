The Rich History of Rome

Founded in 753 BC, Rome was the center of one of the greatest empires of antiquity. With its imposing buildings and monuments, such as the Colosseum and the Roman Forum, the city dominated the ancient world for centuries, leaving a cultural, political, and artistic legacy that has shaped Western civilization. Today, Rome is an open-air museum, where every corner tells its glorious story.
